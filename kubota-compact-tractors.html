Maintenance Tips for Kubota Compact Tractors

Unless the Kubota compact tractor is serviced properly, it is bound to wear out sooner rather than later. Some of the standard maintenance tasks include checking and topping up fluids, examining belts, hoses and greasing fittings, or cleaning and changing air filters. Furthermore, to maximize performance, reliability and durability, it is very important to observe the service intervals specified by the manufacturers and use the original equipment manufacturer and genuine fluids of the Kubota brand.

Furthermore, the operators should not only check the tyres for wear and damage but also check the tyre pressure and inflate it as necessary to achieve the right balance and good grip on the ground. Cleaning the tractor of the rubbish and the dust as usual may also delay the rate of corrosion thereby extending the life of the tractor.
